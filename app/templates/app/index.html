Sama (Sanskrit सम) can be translated to words such as equal, even or balanced. At Sama, we want you to reconnect with your bodies natural sense of evenness and equality. By the time you leave our doors, you will be moving with a greater sense of ease and steadiness. We want to help you achieve the active, pain-free lifestyle that you used to enjoy. Whether you are suffering from an acute or chronic injury, have daily aches and pains, or are mentally or physically stressed out, we are here to help. We will work with you to tailor your sessions to your specific needs. There is no cookie-cutter approach to healing and we will assess your body to determine which techniques will be the most effective.
